🔒 Privacy Policy

We care about your privacy. Here's how your data is handled:

  • We do not collect personal data or track users via servers.
  • All submission data is stored in your browser’s localStorage.
  • No cookies, third-party trackers, or analytics tools are used.
  • Submitted data is visible only on your device unless shared manually.
  • You can delete your data by clearing your browser’s localStorage.
